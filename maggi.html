How to make maggi noodles

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person

Ingredients

1.Maggi-1packet
2.Water-two cups
3.vegetables

Procedure

Step 1: Take two cups of water in a pan.

step 2:Heat the pan on medium flame.

step 3:When the water come to boil,add the noodles cake to the pan.

step 4:Cover it with a lid for a minute.

step 5:After a minute,uncover the lid and addthe tastemaker(maggi masala) to to the pan.

step 6:Add some vegetables to it if you want to.

step 7:Mix it well.

step 8:Without breaking the noodles.

step 9Switch off the flame when all the water boils.

step 10:Enjoy the hot maggi in this lovely weather!
